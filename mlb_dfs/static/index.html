Player tags — what they mean

Form tags (based on recent game logs)
  • 🔥 HOT — last 3 games well above their 14-day average. Streak signal: we weight L3 at 0.9 of projection.
  • 🧊 COLD — last 3 games well below their 14-day average. Projections shrunk an extra 15% post-matchup (calibrated on n>200 days).
  • ⭐ ELITE — L3, L7, L14 all consistently above league average. Always-on producer; no streak dampening.
  • 📊 STEADY — L3/L7/L14 close to each other, around league average. Reliable floor, modest ceiling.
  • untagged — none of the above thresholds tripped. Default projection.
Quality-of-contact (Statcast) tier (true-talent prior, season-long sample)
  • ELITE — barrel% ≥ 11. Top tier of contact quality. Higher floor in slumps.
  • SOLID — above-average barrel/hard-hit. Reliable real production behind the rate stats.
  • AVERAGE — league-average contact. No prior tilt either direction.
  • POOR — below-average barrel/hard-hit. We trust their hot rolling form less; surface stats often regress.
  • — — small Statcast sample (call-up, low BBE), no prior applied.
How they combine

Form tags drive short-term streak weighting (L3 vs L14). Statcast tier drives the true-talent prior blended into the rolling base. A HOT + ELITE hitter is the highest-confidence play — both signals say "buy". A COLD + POOR is the strongest fade — recent form bad, underlying skill weak.

Set up Fantrax cookie auth

Required for private leagues only. Public leagues work without this.

  1. Open fantrax.com in a new tab and make sure you're logged in to your league.
  2. Open DevTools: Cmd+Option+I (Mac) or F12 (Windows).
  3. Go to Application → (left sidebar) Cookies → click https://www.fantrax.com.
  4. Find the rows named FX_RM, JSESSIONID, and gamera_user_id. Copy each Value.
  5. Paste them below as FX_RM=<value>; JSESSIONID=<value>; gamera_user_id=<value> — or just paste the whole cookie table (we'll filter it).

⚠️ The string you paste must contain FX_RM, JSESSIONID, or gamera_user_id — those are the login cookies. Ad/tracking cookies (id5id, panoramaId, pbjs-unifiedid) alone won't work.
The Network tab method can fail on Fantrax because /fxpa/req is often served "from service worker" (cached) with no Cookie: header to copy — the Application → Cookies method above is reliable.

ℹ️ What does "Dyn value" mean?

Dynasty value is a 0–~5000 score = the player's projected value summed over the next 6 seasons (age-curve discounted). It's a relative scale for comparing assets and grading trades — not a points total.
